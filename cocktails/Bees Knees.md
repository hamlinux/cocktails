

## Ingredients:
| Ingredients    | Amount |
| -------------- | ------ |
| London Dry Gin | 1.25   |
| Lemon Juice    | .75    |
| Honey Syrup    | .50    |

### Garnish:
optional
	Cocktail Cherry

### Instructions:
- Combine ingredients in shaker filled with ice.
- Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds
- Double Strain into chilled coupe.
