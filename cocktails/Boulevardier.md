### Description

### Ingredients:
| Ingredients    | Amount |
| -------------- | ------ |
| Bourbon| 1.25   |
| Sweet Vermouth | 1.0       |
| Campari        | 1.0    |

### Garnish:
Orange Twist


### Directions:
-  Combine ingredients over ice in mixing glass. 
- Stir over ice for 20-30 seconds.
- Single Strain into chilled coupe.
- Garnish.
