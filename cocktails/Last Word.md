

## Ingredients:
| Ingredients      | Amount | 
| ---------------- | ------ |
| London Dry Gin   | .75    |
| Green Chartreuse | .75    |
| Marischino Liquer      | .75    |
| Lime Juice       | .75    |

### Garnish
optional
	Cocktail Cherry

### Instructions:
- Combine ingredients in shaker filled with ice.
- Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds
- Double Strain into chilled coupe.
