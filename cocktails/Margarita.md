

## Ingredients:
| Ingredients    | Amount |
| -------------- | ------ |
| Blanco Tequila | 2.0    |
| Orange liquer  | .50    |
| Lime Juice     | 1.0       |
| Agave Syrup | .50    |

### Garnish
Lime Twist	

### Instructions:
Combine ingredients in shaker filled with ice.
Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds
Double Strain into chilled coupe.
