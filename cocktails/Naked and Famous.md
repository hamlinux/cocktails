## Ingredients:
---
| Ingredients                    | Amount |
| ------------------------------ | ------ |
| Del Maguey Chichicapras Mezcal | .75    | 
| Yellow Chartreuse              | .75    |
| Aperol                         | .75    |
| Lime Juice                     | .75    |

###Garnish
	None
	
### Instructions:
Combine ingredients in shaker filled with ice.
Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds
Double Strain into chilled coupe.
