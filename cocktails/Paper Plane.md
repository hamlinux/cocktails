## Ingredients:
| Ingredients      | Amount |
| ---------------- | ------ |
| Bourbon| .75    |
| Aperol| .75    |
| Amaro Nonino       | .75    |
| Lemon Juice       | .75    |

### Garnish
optional
	Paper Plane	

### Instructions:
Combine ingredients in shaker filled with ice.
Shake vigorously for 15-20 seconds
Double Strain into chilled coupe.
